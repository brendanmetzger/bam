Antisocial Networks

It was shortly after George Floyd was murdered, there were protests, and Donald Trump was tweeting whatever nonsense he was tweeting at the moment, and there was Mark Zuckerberg crouching in wait, hoping to be anointed as the capitalist recipient of the advertising dollars tied to Trump's hateful horde of followers.

Amid the protests, I decided it was time to quit platforms. This is an essay on the concept of enabling.
